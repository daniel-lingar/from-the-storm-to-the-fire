## Long Description
**From the Storm to the Fire: The Truth Behind the Silence** is a memoir about what happens when a nervous system learns survival before safety.

Daniel “Bret” Lingar writes from lived experience: premature birth, childhood fear, addiction, grief, family rupture, relationship damage, legal collapse, fatherhood, shame, and the search for language after decades of being misunderstood.

This is not a triumph story with a clean bow. It is a map of the machinery: freeze, fawn, dissociation, hypervigilance, addiction as relief, shame as a second wound, and accountability without self-destruction.

The book does not excuse harm. It explains how survival patterns can become destructive, and why understanding the mechanism is necessary before real repair can begin.
