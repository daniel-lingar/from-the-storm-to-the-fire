# From Testimony to Tools

This book is the testimony.

The **What Really Happened** framework is the toolset built from it.

The purpose is not to turn a life story into a brand slogan. The purpose is to make the hidden sequence visible:

- what happened,
- how the body adapted,
- how survival patterns became costly,
- how shame kept the cycle hidden,
- how language can create a path toward accountability and repair.

The memoir shows the fire.

The framework studies the machinery.

Together, they are meant to help readers, families, peer workers, and organizations understand survival behavior without removing responsibility from the person living it.

Explanation is not excuse.

But without explanation, repair often never reaches the root.
