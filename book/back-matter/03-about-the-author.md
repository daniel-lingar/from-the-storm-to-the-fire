# About the Author

Daniel “Bret” Lingar is a writer and lived-experience educator focused on plain-language survival education, recovery language, and trauma-informed storytelling.

He is the creator of the **What Really Happened** framework, a non-clinical educational approach designed to help readers understand patterns such as shutdown, shame, emotional survival, and repair without relying on academic language.

Bret’s work emphasizes accountability, clarity, and accessibility. His writing is built for people who may not connect with traditional self-help, therapy language, or classroom-style instruction.

He is also involved with Capitol Contracts LLC, where his work focuses on curriculum architecture, technical development, and translating complex lived-experience material into structured educational resources.

**From the Storm to the Fire: The Truth Behind the Silence** is his attempt to turn a difficult life story into language that can help someone else feel less alone.
