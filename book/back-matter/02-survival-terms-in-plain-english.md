This section should help readers understand the repeated survival mechanisms in the book without turning the memoir into a textbook.

## Editorial Rule

Each definition should be short, plain, and human.

Avoid clinical overloading.

Recommended format:

```markdown
## Freeze

Freeze is when the body shuts down because action feels impossible or unsafe. From the outside, it can look like laziness, defiance, or not caring. From the inside, it can feel like being trapped behind glass.
```
